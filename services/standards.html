Service Area

International Standards & Compliance

Standards shape the future of technology, AI governance, and global interoperability. With active participation in leading standards bodies, we help you understand, navigate, and align with the frameworks that matter — today and in the long term.

Bodies We Engage With
ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC42
The primary international AI standards subcommittee — covering AI systems, trustworthiness, data quality, and governance
W3C
World Wide Web Consortium — web standards, accessibility, and semantic technologies
CEN/CLC
European Committee for Standardisation — EU technical and regulatory frameworks
MLCommons
AI benchmarking, safety evaluations, and performance standards for machine learning systems
Linux Foundation
Open-source governance, security, and compliance across the software supply chain
