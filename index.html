About Katamorphic

At Katamorphic Consulting, our mission is to facilitate a productive exchange of information and uncover hidden opporunities in the modern landscape where technology and global engagement converge.

Our ability to support your processes is built on a foundation of multi-disciplinary expertise, including PhDs in AI, data science, software engineering, and cybersecurity, and Masters in Applied Mathematics and Engineering, with experiences across the UK, France, China, the Netherlands, and the US.

We have active engagement with key organisations such as ISO, W3C, CEN/CLC, ForHumanity, and the Linux Foundation to ensure the robustness and compliance, built for the long term. Our perspective is continuously sharpened at the forefront of development, from academic conferences (e.g. ICLR, ACL) to industry-defining summits (e.g. MWC, IBC) and engaging with the core of the open-source community (e.g. OSSEU, FOSDEM).

Our purpose is to help you translate complexity into clarity. Reach out to discover what awaits.
